# Standard Terms by Engagement Type

## Purpose
This document defines the standard contractual terms and conditions that [Organisation Name] IT includes in RFPs by engagement type. These terms serve as the baseline — specific engagements may require additional terms as advised by [Organisation Name] Legal.

---

## Common Terms (All Engagement Types)

### Confidentiality
- All information exchanged during the RFP process and subsequent engagement is confidential
- Partners must execute a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) before contract execution
- Confidentiality obligations survive contract termination for a period of 3 years

### Intellectual Property
- All IP created during the engagement belongs to [Organisation Name] unless explicitly agreed otherwise
- Partner retains ownership of pre-existing IP but grants [Organisation Name] a perpetual, non-exclusive licence to use it in connection with the deliverables

### Data Protection
- Partner must comply with Singapore PDPA and any other applicable data protection regulations
- Personal data must be processed only for the purposes of the engagement
- Data breach notification: within 24 hours of discovery

### Insurance
- Partner must maintain Professional Indemnity insurance of minimum SGD 2,000,000
- Certificate of insurance to be provided before contract execution

### Termination
- Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice (without cause)
- Immediate termination for material breach (with 14 days cure period)
- [Organisation Name] may terminate for convenience with 30 days notice

### Dispute Resolution
- Governing law: Laws of Singapore
- Disputes to be resolved through mediation first, then arbitration under SIAC rules

## Turnkey Project–Specific Terms

### Payment Terms
- Milestone-based payments aligned to project plan
- 10% holdback released upon final acceptance
- Payment within 30 days of approved invoice
- No advance payments

### Acceptance
- Each deliverable subject to formal acceptance testing
- Acceptance period: 10 business days per deliverable
- Defects must be remediated before acceptance

### Warranty
- Minimum 3 months warranty from final project acceptance
- Defects identified during warranty period remediated at no additional cost
- Response time for warranty issues: P1 — 4 hours, P2 — 1 business day

### Delay Penalties
- Liquidated damages of 0.5% of total contract value per week of delay
- Capped at 10% of total contract value
- Force majeure exceptions apply

## Managed Services–Specific Terms

### SLA and Penalties
- SLA targets as defined in the contract schedule
- Service credits for SLA breaches (calculated monthly)
- Persistent SLA failure (3 consecutive months) constitutes material breach

### Transition
- Transition-in: Maximum 30 calendar days, at partner's cost
- Transition-out: Minimum 90 days cooperation period, at agreed rates
- Partner must provide full documentation and knowledge transfer during transition-out

### Contract Duration
- Initial term: [12/24/36 months]
- Renewal: Automatic renewal for 12-month periods unless 90 days notice given
- Annual price review: Maximum increase of CPI + 2%

### Governance
- Monthly service review meetings
- Quarterly business review with senior management
- Annual contract review

---

## Augmented Resources–Specific Terms

### Resource Management
- Replacement guarantee: Underperforming resources replaced within 5 business days
- [Organisation Name] retains right to request replacement with 5 business days notice
- Partner provides minimum 30 days notice before withdrawing a resource

### Rates
- Rates fixed for the initial contract term
- Rate review at renewal only
- Overtime rates: 1.5x standard rate (pre-approved by [Organisation Name] only)

### Working Arrangements
- Resources report to [Organisation Name] line management
- Resources follow [Organisation Name] policies and procedures while on assignment
- Resources are not [Organisation Name] employees — partner remains the employer

### Background Checks
- Partner must conduct background checks on all proposed resources
- Checks include: employment history verification, criminal record check, qualification verification
- Results to be shared with [Organisation Name] before resource commences

---

> **Note:** These are standard terms for RFP purposes. Final contractual terms will be negotiated and agreed with the selected partner and reviewed by [Organisation Name] Legal before execution.
